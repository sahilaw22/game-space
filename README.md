## 🎮 About the Game

**Full Throttle Offroad Truck** is a fast-paced 2D physics-based driving game where every hill, ramp, and broken bridge tests your control and skill. Navigate your powerful offroad truck across dangerous mountain roads, steep climbs, rough terrain, and stunt-heavy levels built for precision and timing.

### Key Features

✨ **Physics-Driven Gameplay** — Realistic truck dynamics with balance and momentum mechanics  
🏔️ **Challenging Levels** — Multiple stages with moving platforms, narrow ledges, and high-risk jumps  
🎯 **Skill-Based Challenge** — Simple controls, hard to master—compete for the best times  
📱 **Mobile Friendly** — Play on desktop, tablet, or smartphone with touch support  
🌄 **Beautiful Visuals** — Hand-crafted 2D landscapes with smooth animations  
🔄 **Infinite Replayability** — Each run tests your precision and reaction time  

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

### Controls

- **Arrow Keys** or **A/D** — Steer left/right
- **Space** or **W** — Accelerate/Jump
- **Balance** — Keep your truck level while climbing hills
- **Momentum** — Use speed to clear gaps without flipping

### Objective

Drive your truck through increasingly difficult courses without flipping. The simple controls make it easy to start, but mastering each level takes focus, timing, and practice.
